## Post 3 - Safety Angle

Image:

```text
assets/x-posts-png/03-safety-gate.png
```

Copy:

```text
Useful autonomy should not mean reckless autonomy.

Alfred can prepare actions:

- draft email
- prepare cart
- research trade
- summarize files
- compare options

But final actions stay gated:

send, pay, trade, publish, merge, delete, share.
```
